Objective :

This project is not just an HTML assignment — it reflects my vision to educate citizens, especially youth and communities, about the Fundamental Rights guaranteed by the Indian Constitution. Through engaging stories and interactive quizzes, the goal is to help people understand, exercise, and protect their rights in everyday life.
